My Top 3 Foods

  1. Chips and Guac

    To start off the list, we have my third favorite food, chips and guac. Chips and guac is a top three favorite food for me for many reasons, but here are a few of the main ones:

    • Tastes amazing!
    • Easy to get, and easy to eat.
    • I can never get tired of it.

  2. Fettuccine Alfredo

    My second favorite food is Fettuccine Alfredo. Here is why I think it is the best version of pasta:

    • Has incredible taste!
    • You can mix many other foods into it including shrimp and chicken.
    • Always leaves you feeling full.

  3. Cheeseburgers

    My number one favorite food is the Cheeseburger. One of America's classics and here is why it is my favorite of all time:

    • One of the best tasting foods out there!
    • Has so many different varieties, you can make an unlimited amount of different kinds of cheeseburgers!
    • Very reliable, you can find this food almost anywhere.
